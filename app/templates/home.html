What is MirtronStructDB?

Mirtrons, a major category of non-canonical micro RNAs (miRNAs), originate from exon-intron boundaries through splicing mechanisms. They are Drosha-independent or Dicer-independent miRNAs. Mirtrons are not limited to animals; they're also found in viruses and plants.

Welcome to MirtronStructDB, where we've curated over 350 novel mirtrons along with structural images, expanding upon existing databases. Explore our database to access valuable information and download what you need.

Start your mirtron journey now and discover the diverse world of these unique miRNAs!
